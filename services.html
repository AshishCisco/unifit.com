Cover Designing

Our endeavour is to design an outstanding cover for all our books. Our experts will give the book cover their creative touch to make sure that it meets the requirements and vision of the author. The original purpose of cover was to protect inside pages and to bind. Now that we design it, then why not make it an exceptional one! A cover must be such that it speaks volume to the one holding the book.


Premium Cover – Rs. 2500

Customised Cover – Rs. 5000 onwards

Interior Designing

In book designing, page layout refers to the process of placing and arranging text, images, and graphics on a software page. The appropriate book size, font size, style, margin, etc. all are essential to design a book which is beautiful to look at and easy and pleasant to read. All this requires expertise, which we have and offer.


Basic Interior (Only text and one design element) – Rs. 3,000 (for a book of up to 150 pages)


Premium Interior (Text, images, figures, tables and various design elements options) – Rs. 5,000 onwards (for a book of up to 150 pages)

Copy Editing

Copyediting is an exercise wherein the editor checks for any spelling, grammar, punctuation errors and review terminology, semantics and jargon. Editor also ensures that the idea the writer wishes to portray is clear and easy to understand, and uninterrupted.


Basic Copyediting (grammar and syntax) – Re 0.40 per word


Comprehensive/Detailed Copyediting (restructure sentences wherever necessary) – Re 0.70 per word

Proofreading Service

It is the process of checking the final draft of a book to ensure consistency and accuracy in grammar, spelling, punctuation, and formatting. The errors that might have been overlooked during the stage of copyediting are taken care of at this stage. The reviewing of content at the last stage helps in taking us a step nearer to an error-free content.


Charges – Re 0.20 per word.
