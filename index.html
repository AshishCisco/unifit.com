We are Professional Book Publishers

The inception of Imprint Publishers is the result of innovative and agile minds. We are a team of competent and professional members. With an experience of over 5 years in the book publishing industry, we have an exhaustive understanding of the world of publishing, which makes us aware of the troubles and problems which authors face and hence, we are here, to provide solutions.


“There’s a lot more to publishing a book than writing it and slapping a cover on it.” — Vince Flynn

We, at Imprint Publishers, believe this. The whole production process, from editing, designing the cover, formatting the pages, printing, distributing and marketing, every step contributes in making the book a finished product to be launched in the market. This is a platform to make the journey of publishing easy and worthy.
