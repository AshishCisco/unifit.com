Readymade Uniforms in UAE

We also provide readymade uniforms to our customers which include Tshirt, Lab Coat, Napkins etc. Additionally embroidery, screen printing, sublimation or heat transfer printing options can be used for personalising or highlighting your brand.

PPE Products

We have a collection of ready made PPE kits. We manufacture and sell antibacterial Personal Protective Equipments (PPE) to customers in different GSM qualities as per demand from customers with the various customizations option in designs and accessories. Our PPE kits comprises coverall, medical or isolation gown, disposable facemasks, reissuable facemasks, disposable hijabs, head cover and shoe covers as well. We can also manufacture other protective gadgets like curtains, pillow covers, bed sheets, table covers etc as per client requirements.

Tshirts

We have a variety of ready made Shirt & Hoodie collections at reasonable prices. Further these can be customized using various options like embroidery, screen printing and sublimations. We have collections of dry fit, honeycomb, cotton, poly mixture fabrics, t-shirts and hoodies which come in round neck, V-neck and with color options.
